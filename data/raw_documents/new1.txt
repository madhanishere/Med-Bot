### PATIENT RIGHTS & RESPONSIBILITIES

Patient Rights:
- Every patient has the right to receive respectful and dignified care regardless of caste, religion, gender, or financial status.
- Patients have the right to be informed about their diagnosis, treatment options, risks, and expected outcomes in a language they understand.
- Patients have the right to give or refuse informed consent before any procedure or surgery.
- Patients have the right to privacy and confidentiality of their medical records.
- Patients have the right to seek a second opinion from another specialist within or outside the hospital.
- Patients have the right to view their medical records and receive copies upon request (processing time: 3 working days).
- Patients have the right to file a complaint or grievance without fear of discrimination.

Patient Responsibilities:
- Patients must provide accurate and complete information about their health history, medications, and allergies.
- Patients are responsible for following the treatment plan as advised by the doctor.
- Patients must respect hospital staff, other patients, and hospital property.
- Patients must adhere to hospital rules, visiting hours, and infection control guidelines.
- Patients must settle all billing dues at the time of discharge.
- Patients must inform the nursing staff if they choose to leave against medical advice (AMA).

### DISCHARGE POLICY
- Discharge orders are typically issued in the morning. Patients are requested to complete discharge formalities by 12:00 PM.
- Final bill preparation may take 2–4 hours after discharge order is issued.
- Insurance cashless discharge may take additional 1–2 hours for insurer approval.
- Patients requiring ambulance or patient transport must inform the nursing station at least 2 hours before discharge.
- Discharge summary and prescriptions will be provided at the time of discharge. Digital copy can be emailed on request.
- For early morning discharge, please inform nursing staff the previous evening.
- Patients who leave without completing discharge formalities will be considered as Against Medical Advice (AMA) discharge.

### GRIEVANCE & FEEDBACK POLICY
- Patients and attendants are encouraged to share feedback through the Feedback Forms available at all nursing stations and the reception.
- Online feedback can be submitted at feedback@hospital.com.
- A Patient Relations Officer (PRO) is available Monday to Saturday, 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM at the reception (Ground Floor, Block A).
- Grievances will be acknowledged within 24 hours and resolved within 7 working days.
- For urgent complaints, contact the Duty Manager available 24/7 at the main reception.
- An independent Patient Grievance Committee meets on the last Friday of every month to review unresolved complaints.
