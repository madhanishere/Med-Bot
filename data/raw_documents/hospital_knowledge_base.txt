4. VISITOR RULES & REGULATIONS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

GENERAL VISITING HOURS:
  Morning Visit  : 8:00 AM – 10:00 AM
  Evening Visit  : 5:00 PM – 7:00 PM
  ICU / NICU     : 9:00 AM – 10:00 AM and 6:00 PM – 7:00 PM (10 minutes only)

VISITOR PASS:
  - All visitors must collect a Visitor Pass from the Reception Desk.
  - A valid government-issued photo ID (Aadhaar, Voter ID, Passport) is mandatory.
  - Pass must be worn visibly at all times within the hospital premises.
  - Passes are non-transferable.

NUMBER OF VISITORS ALLOWED:
  - General Ward        : Maximum 2 visitors per patient at a time
  - Private / Semi-Private Room : Maximum 2 visitors at a time
  - ICU / NICU / CCU    : Maximum 1 attendant (immediate family only)
  - Operation Theatre (OT) waiting: 1 family member permitted in designated waiting area

RESTRICTED AREAS (No visitors allowed):
  - Intensive Care Unit (ICU) – except during designated hours
  - Neonatal ICU (NICU)
  - Operation Theatre (OT) and Recovery Room
  - Sterile zones and Procedure Rooms
  - Pharmacy, Laboratory, and Radiology rooms

CHILDREN VISITORS:
  - Children below 12 years are not permitted inside wards or ICU.
  - Children must remain in the designated waiting area with an adult guardian.

CONDUCT & HYGIENE:
  - Visitors must sanitize hands at the entrance of every ward.
  - Face masks are recommended; mandatory for ICU and isolation wards.
  - Loud talking, use of mobile phones near medical equipment, and smoking are strictly prohibited.
  - No outside food is allowed in ICU, NICU, and isolation rooms.
  - Alcohol and tobacco are strictly prohibited anywhere on hospital premises.

ATTENDANT POLICY:
  - One designated attendant is permitted to stay overnight with the patient.
  - Attendant must register at the ward nurse station and carry a valid ID.
  - Attendant cot / recliner chair will be provided on request.
  - Additional attendants may be asked to leave after visiting hours.

PHOTOGRAPHY & RECORDING:
  - Photography or video recording of patients, staff, or medical procedures is strictly prohibited without written consent.
  - Violation may result in removal from premises.

GIFTS & FLOWERS:
  - Fresh flowers are not permitted in ICU and NICU areas due to infection risk.
  - Food items from outside are permitted in general wards only if sealed and commercially packaged.

  - Corporate Billing Desk: Ground Floor, Counter No. 5
  - For new empanelment enquiries: billing@citycarehosp.in

ADVANCE DEPOSIT POLICY:
  - An advance deposit is required at the time of admission for planned procedures.
  - General Ward Admission Advance   : ₹5,000
  - Semi-Private / Private Room      : ₹10,000
  - Deluxe / Suite Room              : ₹15,000 – ₹25,000
  - ICU Admission Advance            : ₹20,000
  - Surgery / Procedure Advance      : As per estimated cost (provided in writing)
  - Advance amount is adjusted against final bill at discharge.

DISCHARGE & FINAL BILLING:
  - Discharge request must be placed with the ward nurse or doctor.
  - Final bill is generated within 2–4 hours of discharge request.
  - Bill review and clarification: Billing Counter, Ground Floor
  - Itemized bill available on request.
  - Discharge summary and medical records issued at discharge.

REFUNDS:
  - Excess advance amount is refunded at the time of discharge.
  - Refunds via original payment mode (cash refund for cash payments; online refund for card/UPI within 5–7 working days).
  - Insurance refund (if pre-auth exceeds actual bill): processed within 15 working days.
