### DOCUMENTS REQUIRED FOR REGISTRATION
New Patient Registration:
- Valid government-issued photo ID (Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, Passport, Voter ID, or Driving License)
- One recent passport-size photograph
- Contact number and address proof

For Follow-up Visits:
- Hospital Patient ID card (issued at first registration)
- Previous prescription or discharge summary

For Admission (In-patient):
- All OP registration documents
- Attendant/guardian ID proof
- Insurance card or company authorization letter (if applicable)

For Emergency Admission:
- Documents can be submitted after stabilization
- Attendant must submit ID within 24 hours

For Minors (Below 18):
- Parent or guardian's ID proof
- Birth certificate of the patient

Insurance Process:
1. Visit the insurance desk with your insurance card and valid ID
2. Submit pre-authorization request (for planned procedures)
3. Cashless facility available for network hospitals
4. Reimbursement claims processed within 15 working days
5. For emergencies, insurance desk can be contacted at any time via duty manager

Important Notes:
- Pre-authorization required for surgeries and planned admissions (minimum 48 hours in advance)
- For cashless treatment, bring your insurance e-card or physical card
- OPD visits may not be covered — check your policy

### VISITOR RULES & REGULATIONS
General Visiting Hours: 10:00 AM to 12:00 PM and 5:00 PM to 7:00 PM
ICU Visiting Hours: 11:00 AM to 11:30 AM and 5:00 PM to 5:30 PM (strictly 30 minutes)
Neonatal ICU (NICU): Parents only, 10:00 AM to 11:00 AM and 4:00 PM to 5:00 PM

Visitor Rules:
- Maximum 2 visitors per patient at a time (ICU: 1 visitor only)
- Children below 12 years not allowed in ICU/NICU or general wards
- Visitor passes must be collected from reception desk
- Mobile phones must be on silent mode in all clinical areas
- Photography and videography strictly prohibited
- Outside food not allowed in ICU and post-surgery wards
- Maintain hygiene — use hand sanitizers provided at all entry points
- Visitors with cold, fever, or any infections advised not to visit
- Visitor parking available in Basement Level B1 and B2
