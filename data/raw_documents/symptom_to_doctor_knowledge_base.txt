SECTION 1: HEART & CHEST RELATED ISSUES
================================================================================

SYMPTOMS / ISSUES:
  - Chest pain or chest tightness
  - Shortness of breath (at rest or on exertion)
  - Heart palpitations / irregular heartbeat / racing heart
  - High blood pressure (hypertension)
  - High cholesterol / lipid problems
  - Swelling in legs with breathlessness
  - Heart attack follow-up care
  - Angioplasty / bypass surgery follow-up
  - Dizziness or fainting spells related to heart
  - Family history of heart disease (preventive check)
  - Echocardiography (2D Echo) needed

SECTION 2: BRAIN, NERVES & SPINE RELATED ISSUES
================================================================================

SYMPTOMS / ISSUES:
  - Severe or frequent headaches / migraines
  - Dizziness, vertigo, or loss of balance
  - Numbness or tingling in hands, feet, or face

SECTION 20: EMERGENCY SITUATIONS — GO TO EMERGENCY DIRECTLY
================================================================================

Do NOT wait for an appointment. Go to Emergency or call +91-44-2800-9999
for ANY of the following:

  🚨 Chest pain with sweating (possible heart attack)
  🚨 Sudden loss of consciousness or fainting
  🚨 Stroke signs: facial drooping, arm weakness, speech difficulty
  🚨 Difficulty breathing or choking
  🚨 Severe allergic reaction (anaphylaxis)
  🚨 High fever with fits / seizures
  🚨 Uncontrolled bleeding (trauma or internal)
  🚨 Suicidal thoughts or self-harm attempt
  🚨 Sudden loss of vision
  🚨 Severe burns or poisoning
  🚨 Road accident or major trauma
  🚨 Diabetic emergency (unconscious, very low blood sugar)
  🚨 Child with high fever and stiff neck

Emergency is open 24 hours, 7 days a week, 365 days a year.
Ambulance: +91-44-2800-1122
